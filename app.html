Gestures Manual

  • Paint: Raise only your index finger.
  • Air-Click: Move cursor over a button and pinch (index + thumb) to click.
  • Pinky Eraser: Raise only your pinky finger to toggle the eraser.
  • Clear Canvas: Hold an open palm (all 5 fingers) still for 1.5 seconds.
  • Undo / Redo: With an open palm, quickly swipe left to Undo, or right to Redo.
  • Dynamic Brush: Move your hand closer to the camera for a thicker brush.

Other Apps

  • AI Finger Counter: Hold up any number of fingers and keep your hand steady to hear the AI count them.
  • Rock Paper Scissors: Select RPS from the dropdown, click 'Play Match', and throw your gesture on "SHOOT!".
  • Tic-Tac-Toe & Calculator: Move your index finger to control the cursor. Pinch (index + thumb) to air-click buttons.
  • Picture Puzzle: Hold up 5 fingers to snap a picture. Then pinch, hold, and drag a tile to slide it into the empty space.
